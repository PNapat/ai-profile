# Projects

## Project 1: Twitter Sentiment Analysis of Bangkok Tourism During COVID-19 Pandemic Using Support Vector Machine Algorithm
- **What it is:** Journal of Disaster Research publication analyzing Twitter sentiment about Bangkok tourism during the COVID-19 pandemic.
- **Problem it solves:** Helps tourism stakeholders understand public sentiment during a severe disruption to travel and hospitality.
- **Tech stack:** NLP, sentiment analysis, support vector machine, Twitter data.
- **My role:** Co-author and research contributor.
- **Outcome / impact:** Published in Journal of Disaster Research, 2021.
- **Link:** https://doi.org/10.20965/jdr.2021.p0024

## Project 2: Analyzing Adolescents' E-cigarette Initiation Tendency Using Explainable Machine Learning
- **What it is:** Journal of Computational Social Science publication using explainable machine learning to identify factors associated with youth e-cigarette curiosity and susceptibility.
- **Problem it solves:** Provides interpretable evidence for public health prevention and survey design.
- **Tech stack:** Explainable AI, Random Forest, XGBoost, SHAP, National Youth Tobacco Survey data.
- **My role:** Lead author and researcher.
- **Outcome / impact:** Published in Journal of Computational Social Science, 2025.
- **Link:** https://doi.org/10.1007/s42001-025-00423-6
